# 01 — GoPro HERO13 Black Landing Research

## 1. Project Context

**Project name:** Official-style GoPro HERO13 Black landing concept  
**Format:** Portfolio / production-style marketing landing page  
**Product:** GoPro HERO13 Black  
**Goal:** Create the feeling of a real product preorder/promo page, but keep it clearly as a portfolio concept, not an official GoPro website.

The landing should feel like a premium/extreme-sports product page: cinematic scroll, large 3D product presence, short punchy copy, strong CTA, and a clear comparison that shows why a dedicated action camera is better than a normal smartphone for action/extreme scenarios.

> Important: this is an unofficial portfolio concept. Do not present it as the real GoPro website, do not accept payments, and do not store real customer data.

## 3. Core Positioning

### Main idea

A phone can record a moment. HERO13 Black is built to survive inside the moment.

### Best positioning angles

1. **Action-first camera**  
   The camera is made for movement, speed, water, dust, mounts, helmets, bikes, snowboards, surfboards, and hands-free capture.

2. **Footage-first experience**  
   Highlight 5.3K60, 4K120, slow motion, stabilization, vertical/social-ready framing, and 10-bit/HDR workflow.

3. **Ruggedness over convenience**  
   A phone is convenient in your pocket; GoPro is safer to mount, easier to use in extreme movement, and less risky than exposing your daily phone.

4. **Modular creativity**  
   HB-Series lenses create a strong visual story: Ultra Wide POV, Macro detail, Anamorphic cinematic shots, ND motion blur.

5. **Preorder motivation**  
   The landing does not need real checkout. It needs to create the feeling of early access: “Reserve your spot”, “Get notified”, “Be first to ride with HERO13 Black”.

## 4. Comparison With a Typical Phone Below HERO13 Black Budget

Chosen comparison model: **Samsung Galaxy A25 5G**.

Why this phone is a good comparison:

- It is a recognizable 2024 budget/mid-range Android phone.
- Its US price was around **$299**, below the HERO13 Black launch price of **$399.99**.
- It has a modern 50MP OIS camera and 4K video, so the comparison is not unfair against a weak phone.
- It represents the real question users may ask: “Why buy a separate action camera if my phone already records video?”

Sources used:

- Samsung official Galaxy A25 5G page: https://www.samsung.com/uk/business/smartphones/galaxy-a/galaxy-a25-5g-blue-128gb-sm-a256bzbdeub/
- EE Galaxy A25 5G specifications: https://deviceguides.ee.co.uk/samsung/galaxy-a25-5g-android-14/specifications/
- Android Authority Galaxy A25 5G review and price: https://www.androidauthority.com/samsung-galaxy-a25-review-3421050/

### Comparison table

| Category | GoPro HERO13 Black | Typical cheaper 2024 phone: Galaxy A25 5G | Advantage to highlight |
|---|---|---|---|
| Price context | $399.99 launch price | ~$299 US price | Phone is cheaper, but not action-built. |
| Main use | Dedicated action camera | Everyday smartphone | HERO13 is purpose-built for sport capture. |
| Video | 5.3K60, 4K120, high-frame-rate modes | 4K30, 1080p30 listed in specs | HERO13 gives smoother action and stronger slow motion. |
| Stabilization | HyperSmooth 6.0 in-camera stabilization | OIS/VDIS; 4K stabilization can be limited depending on mode/review | HERO13 is built around movement. |
| Waterproofing | 33ft / 10m waterproof | Budget phone is not positioned as a serious action/water camera | HERO13 is safer for water, mud, snow, surf. |
| Mounting | Magnetic latch, GoPro mounts, tripod thread | Handheld/phone clamp | HERO13 is easier to mount on helmet, bike, chest, board. |
| Size/risk | Small dedicated camera | Larger daily phone | Less risk than strapping your main phone to action gear. |
| Creative lenses | HB-Series lens mods with auto-detect | Fixed phone cameras | HERO13 has stronger modular action POV options. |
| GPS overlays | Speed, altitude, path, g-force in Quik | General GPS phone data | HERO13 makes sports telemetry part of the video story. |
| Handling | Designed for one-button/action workflows | Touchscreen phone workflow | Better for gloves, wet hands, movement, mounting. |

### Main comparison message

Do not present the smartphone as “bad”. The phone is cheaper and good for everyday content. The landing should say:

> A phone is great for daily moments. HERO13 Black is for the moments where your phone should stay in your pocket.

## 5. Landing Message Architecture

### Main promise

**Built for the shot your phone cannot safely take.**

### Supporting messages

- Capture full-speed action in 5.3K60 and 4K120.
- Keep footage stable with HyperSmooth 6.0.
- Go underwater, into mud, snow, dust, and impact-risk environments.
- Mount it anywhere: helmet, bike, board, tripod, chest, car.
- Switch creative perspectives with HB-Series lenses.
- Create social-ready shots from full-frame footage.
- Add speed, altitude, path, and g-force overlays after the session.

## 7. Data Collection Simulation

The site should not save real user data. It should only simulate data collection in the frontend.

### Data points to simulate

- Email/contact typed into form.
- Selected bundle/version.
- CTA click.
- Source/UTM idea.
- Scroll depth.
- Transition to main website.
- Interaction with key blocks or 3D model.

### Required behavior

After preorder form submit:

1. Validate the email field visually.
2. Clear the contact field.
3. Show a thank-you message.
4. Say that the team will contact the user by email.
5. Do not store data in database, API, localStorage, cookies, or analytics.

Recommended thank-you text:

> Thanks for your preorder request. We’ll contact you soon by email with the next steps.

## 8. Marketing Copy Direction

The user selected: **general marketing copy**.

Tone:

- Strong.
- Short.
- Extreme-sports oriented.
- Product-first.
- Premium, but not overloaded.
- Less technical explanation, more clear benefit.

Example lines:

- **Built for the shot your phone cannot safely take.**
- **Mount it. Send it. Capture everything.**
- **5.3K clarity for full-speed moments.**
- **Stable when the ground is not.**
- **Water, snow, mud, speed — bring it on.**
- **Keep your phone in your pocket. Put HERO13 Black in the action.**

---

## 9. Visual / Interaction References

The landing should take inspiration from official premium product pages:

- Full-screen sections.
- Large product imagery or 3D model.
- Short text blocks near the product.
- Scroll-driven reveal.
- Pinned sections.
- Feature-by-feature storytelling.
- Strong CTA repetition.
- Minimal navigation.

Avoid:

- Heavy particle effects.
- Overloaded UI.
- Progress bar.
- Long text walls.
- Fake checkout/payment.
- Storing real personal data.

---

## 10. Research Conclusion

The strongest angle for this portfolio landing is not “GoPro has better specs than a phone” in a dry way. The stronger angle is:

> HERO13 Black is the camera for situations where a phone becomes inconvenient, risky, unstable, or physically unsuitable.

The landing should make this obvious through scroll scenes: action, stabilization, mounting, water, modular lenses, and a direct comparison against a cheaper but capable everyday smartphone.
